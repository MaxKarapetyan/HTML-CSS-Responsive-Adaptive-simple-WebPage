about us

The company was founded in 2019. We started our activity as a training center, developing and training excellent engineers, who later joined our team. Goya stands out in the market with its non-formal education, giving students in-depth knowledge in the field of professional software development DevOps and QA. We specialize in many areas and always hear satisfying feedback from our customers.
